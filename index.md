# Privacy Policy for BeamDocs

**Last updated:** April 2026

Rafael Paula ("we", "us", or "our") built the BeamDocs app as a Free app. This SERVICE is provided by Rafael Paula at no cost and is intended for use as is.

This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information if anyone decided to use our Service.

## 1. Local Storage (Your Documents)
**All scanned documents, PDFs, and images are stored locally on your device.** 
We do not upload, sync, or store your documents on any external servers. Your files remain completely private and under your control.

## 2. Information Collection and Use
We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII). However, the app uses third-party services that may collect anonymous data used to identify crashes and improve the app:

*   **Firebase Analytics:** We collect anonymous usage data (e.g., how often the scan feature is used) to help us understand how the app is performing and where we can improve it.
*   **Firebase Crashlytics:** We collect anonymous crash reports to help us fix bugs and improve the stability of the app.

We **do not** sell, rent, or share this data with any third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

## 3. Security
We value your trust in providing us your information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. The app strictly enforces HTTPS for any network communication (e.g., sending crash logs to Google Firebase) to ensure data integrity and security.

## 4. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
